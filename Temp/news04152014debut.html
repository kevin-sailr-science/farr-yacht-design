Farr 280 has successful debut

Published April 15, 2014

Chessie Racing placed first in the PHRF B division at Charleston Race Week 2014. It placed first in four out of the six races. The regatta featured light winds over all three days. The boat performed well while sailing downwind, where it was able to sail faster than the other 10 boats in the fleet.

 

The boat arrived in Charleston just a few days before the regatta. Premier Composite Technologies, builders of the Farr 280, were able to send the boat by cargo plane from Dubai to the regatta. The team racing the boat had just a few days to practice and get the boat fully setup. Sailing went well during practice and also during the regatta. See photos via Facebook and also regatta videos.

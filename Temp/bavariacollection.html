Bavaria Yachts

Together with Bavaria Yachtbau, Farr Yacht Design has designed  14 different models since 2009. These include the Cruiser and Vision lines, along with the B/One sportboat. Our designs have won multiple Cruising World Boat of the Year, European Yacht of the Year and SAIL Best Boat awards.

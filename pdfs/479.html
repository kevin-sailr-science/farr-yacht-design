Horizon F14 Paddleboard

A 14ft. paddleboard, designed for flatwater and estuary races. Features a wave-piercing bow and a subtle peak along the centerline of the deck, allowing the bow to quickly shed water and return to the surface.  We have produced 2 prototype boards, and have gone through an extensive sea trialling period. We have been very encouraged by the initial test results against many of the top competitors, and find that our targets have been well validated. We are working to create a refinement of that board that incorporates some minor deck geometry variations, and intend to have a third prototype completed shortly before expanding into initial production.

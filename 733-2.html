Design #733 Bavaria Vision 46


The offset companionway allows for a cockpit with a dedicated entertaining area.  The cockpit table can also be lowered to create a double bunk.  The steering is split to allow comfortable passage to the fold down swim platform.  Numerous storage areas aft allow owners many options for the necessities of life aboard.  

The coaming contains primary winches and optional secondary winches with lines for the mainsheet, vang and jib sheets led within easy reach while sitting at the helm.  Beneath each helm seat is an opening bin for line storage.  The jib sheet tracks are submerged in the top of the cabin house to allow easy passage and movement about deck.  Moving forward, the anchor well features a submerged anchor roller and roller furler in keeping with the cleanliness of wide chainplates and flush hatches.  A separate bow sprit is optional and can be easily fitted to the port side for flying cruising spinnakers.  The hull to deck joint is also hidden with only a teak toe rail to keep one secure while moving about.

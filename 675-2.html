Design #675 Bavaria Cruiser 32

The new Cruiser 32 has been developed by the renowned designer, Farr Yacht Design, just like her sister ship the Cruiser 55. The smallest member of the New Classic family offers an omptimized, modern bow with sportive performance as well as a spacious interior concept.

Some of the main features found the Cruiser 55 have been incorporated in to the new Cruiser 32 which will make a dream boat for all who enjoy sailing with the whole family. And as with any Bavaria, the value for money is unbeatable.

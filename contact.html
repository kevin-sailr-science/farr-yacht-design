Before You Reach Out

Having a few details ready helps us give you a better answer faster:

  • New design: Intended use, approximate LOA, timeline, budget range
  • Consulting: Boat class, rating rule, current certificate if available
  • Design plans: Design number or name, intended use
  • Superyacht: Size range, intended use, build timeline

Don’t worry if you don’t have all the details — that’s what the conversation is for.

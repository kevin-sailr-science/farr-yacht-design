Volvo Open 70 Telefonica Blue (2)

70’ | Volvo Open 70 | 2007
◆ 2008–09 Volvo Ocean Race

The second Telefonica entry in the 2008–09 Volvo Ocean Race completed a two-boat Spanish campaign that made Telefonica the most prominent commercial programme in that edition of the race. The twin Farr Open 70 designs gave Spain exceptional depth of preparation, with both boats providing data and performance benchmarks against each other during build and training.

Running two boats to the same design is an expensive but strategically powerful approach in offshore racing: it accelerates sail and equipment development, provides crew depth, and creates race-within-a-race competition that sharpens everyone involved. The Telefonica programme executed this approach at the highest level.

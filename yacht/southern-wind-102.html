Southern Wind 102

102’ | Performance Superyacht | 2010
◆ Southern Wind Shipyard Partnership

The Southern Wind 102 represents a long-standing partnership between Farr Yacht Design and Southern Wind Shipyard in Cape Town. Southern Wind produces high-performance sailing yachts in the 82–115-foot range, and the 102 is among the most successful models in their production line — fast enough to be competitive in superyacht racing while delivering the interior volume and build quality expected of a yacht in this class.

The SW102 combines offshore capability with regatta performance, making it a natural choice for owners who want a boat that can cross oceans in comfort and then compete at events like the St. Barths Bucket and the Superyacht Cup. Multiple SW102 hulls have been launched to the Farr design, establishing a strong class within the Southern Wind production range.

The Infiniti 60 from Farr Yacht Design has been developed as a high performance sailing yacht that provides the ability either to day sail shorthanded or to race fully crewed at levels of performance never previously seen in a yacht of this genre.

 

The Infiniti 60 is presented in two versions: the Infiniti 60R is a pure racing yacht with optional manual winch systems while the Infiniti 60GT has powered systems, and the option of a lifting keel. The DNA of both yachts remains pure performance and balance due to the patented Dynamic Stability Systems® retractable lateral foil.

 

The Infiniti 60 is intended to redefine performance sailing in comfort, delivering unequaled speed, balance and versatility; performance is paramount.

Partners

Builder Partnerships

World-class builders. Farr-designed hulls. The partnerships that have produced race-winning superyachts, iconic one-design classes, and the best-selling production racers of their era.

Southern Wind Yachts

Cape Town-based builder of performance sailing superyachts. Farr’s most enduring superyacht partnership, producing six model lines from the SW82 to the SW110. Multiple St. Barths Bucket class victories.

View Southern Wind designs →

Baltic Yachts

Finland’s premier custom superyacht builder. Home of the Baltic 142 — Kiboko 4, World Superyacht Award winner 2024 — and the DSS-foiling Baltic Custom 142. The standard for premium carbon superyacht construction.

View Baltic designs →

Nautor Swan

Finnish builder of premium cruiser-racers and superyachts. Nautor Swan’s collaboration with Farr produced offshore-capable racing platforms trusted by serious programs worldwide.

View Swan designs →

Carroll Marine

Rhode Island-based builder of the original Farr 40 — the one-design class that has attracted 152+ hulls and continues racing in international championships across three continents.

View Farr 40 →

Cookson Boats

Auckland-based builder of the Cookson 50 — 30+ hulls — and other Farr offshore designs. Cookson’s carbon construction expertise and Farr’s design methodology produced one of the most successful offshore racing classes of the 2000s.

View Cookson designs →

McConaghy Boats

High-performance specialist builder with facilities in Australia and China. McConaghy’s advanced composite construction has been applied to several Farr grand prix and one-design programs, producing fast, light race boats built to professional standards.

View racing designs →

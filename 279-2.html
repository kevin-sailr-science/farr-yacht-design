Design #279 - FARR IMS 39

The Farr IMS 39 is a fast, strong, comfortable and most of all fun and rewarding to sail. It is
one example in a range of IMS designs developed by Bruce Farr & Associates, along with the
Farr IMS 40, 44 and 50. These designs offer the versatility to suit the requirements of owners
whether they choose to focus on racing with the opportunity to cruise comfortably , or cruising
with the option to race competitively.

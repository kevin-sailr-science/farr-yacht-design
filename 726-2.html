Design #726 Bavaria Cruiser 36

The CRUISER 36 includes a choice of three wood alternatives for the interior (mahogany, light oak & walnut) and a total 12 different upholstery options.

Much emphasis has been placed on the roominess under deck. In addition to the package efficiency the roominess is supported by the light and air concepts. For more brightness we integrated five hull and six portlights supported by seven flush hatches, translucent companionway sliding hatch and LED illumination which is very energy efficient. In order to achieve good airflow under deck the 36 has seven open able deck portlights each having mosquito and sun shades as standard.

The sailing performance and handling of the CRUISER 36 is a major step forward compared to its predecessor and to competition. To cut it short, the CRUISER 36 offers quality, safety and many features as seen on her larger sister ships

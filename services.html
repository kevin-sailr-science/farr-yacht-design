What We Do

Six disciplines under one roof — everything a yacht needs, from concept to campaign.

⚓

Naval Architecture

Hull shapes, appendage design, and sail plans — the core of every Farr yacht. Our approach combines computational fluid dynamics with five decades of design intuition to deliver hulls that perform across the full wind range.

◆

Structural Engineering

Lightweight structures that meet classification society requirements without compromising performance. From carbon racing builds to aluminum expedition yachts, we engineer structures that are as efficient as the hulls they support.

✎

Design Detail & Drafting

Complete drawing sets, weight calculations, hardware specification, and build documentation. Our drafting team delivers production-ready packages that give builders everything they need to start cutting material.

⚙

Research & Development

Tank testing, wind tunnel programs, CFD analysis, and VPP development. When a campaign demands an edge, our R&D capability delivers answers that can’t be found in a spreadsheet.

★

Consulting

Rating optimization, performance analysis, and campaign strategy. Whether you’re chasing a certificate advantage or preparing for a round-the-world campaign, our consulting services put data behind every decision.

Lifecycle Support

Re-engineering, appendage redesign, certificate reviews, and refit consulting. Farr designs stay competitive for decades — and we’re here to make sure they keep performing at their best.

Award January 2025

Kiboko 4 Wins World Superyacht Award

The Farr-designed Baltic 142 Kiboko 4 received the World Superyacht Award in the sailing superyacht category, recognising the boat as the most significant sailing superyacht of its era. The award confirms Farr’s continued relevance at the highest level of performance superyacht design.

Racing October 2024

American Magic Campaign — AC37

Farr Yacht Design contributed to the American Magic AC37 campaign, engaging with the most technically demanding yacht design challenge of the era: foiling monohulls reaching 50+ knots in the America’s Cup 37th Match in Barcelona.

Award March 2024

Gelliceaux Wins St. Barths Bucket

Gelliceaux, a Farr-designed Southern Wind 108, won her class at the St. Barths Bucket Regatta 2024 — the premier superyacht racing event in the Caribbean. The result added another major trophy to the Farr-Southern Wind partnership’s already distinguished record.

Studio 2019

Britton Ward Named President

Britton Ward was named President of Farr Yacht Design, bringing fresh leadership to the office while maintaining the design methodology and performance standards that have defined the practice since 1969. Ward joined the office in 2003 and has been involved in the office’s most recent projects.

Launch 2020

Southern Wind 96 Delivered

The Farr-designed Southern Wind 96 was delivered to her owners in 2020 and immediately began a successful racing campaign, recording class results at the St. Barths Bucket and other premier superyacht regattas. The SW96 is the most recent production model in the Farr-Southern Wind partnership.
